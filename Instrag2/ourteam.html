OUR TEAM

Public affairs specialists with an expertise acquired over years of battling in the front lines of disruption,
building bridges with regulators, and partnering with communities to bring about transformative change.

Juan Mauricio Mora

Has been Director of Public Policy for Mexico, Central America and the Caribbean for Uber, Chief lobbyist for COPARMEX, and has held different positions in the Mexican Federal Government, including Strategic Advisor to the Secretary of Public Administration. Juan Mauricio has also held important position’s at Partido Acción Nacional, including National Secretary of Civic Outreach at PAN’s National Committee. Juan Mauricio holds an MPP from the University of Chicago, MSc in Democracy and Democratization from University College London, and a BA in Law from ITAM.

Oscar Gastelum

Has been Managing Partner for Mexico and Latin America of GSV Equity, Project Coordinator of the Federal Agency for Trade and Investment (PROMEXICO), Private Secretary of the Governor of the State of Sonora. He holds a Masters of Business Administration from the W.P. CAREY School of Business of Arizona State University, a Mayor in Finance from the Tec de Monterrey in Mexico.
